Duterte war on drugs

Since June 30 until October 10 in Philippines, 1135 people were shot according to official records. Unofficial records claim about 3500 victims in so-called "War on Drugs" extrajudicial killings initiated by president Duterte 2016-2019.

This projects aim to visualize the data associated with the operations.
